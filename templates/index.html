Webhook Setup for Auto-Reply

1. Meta Developer Console → Webhooks
2. Subscribe: instagram → comments and instagram → messages
3. Callback URL: https://your-domain/webhook
4. Verify Token: your WEBHOOK_VERIFY_TOKEN
