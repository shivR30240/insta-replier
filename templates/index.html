DM Permissions Required

Add these in Meta Developer Console → Your App → Permissions:
instagram_manage_messages · pages_messaging

Webhook Setup

1. Meta Developer Console → Your App → Webhooks
2. Subscribe to instagram → comments (for comment auto-reply)
3. Subscribe to instagram → messages (for DM auto-reply)
4. Callback URL: https://your-domain.com/webhook
5. Verify Token: same as WEBHOOK_VERIFY_TOKEN in .env
